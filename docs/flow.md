# Overall Business Flow

This outlines the end-to-end operational flow used as the product backbone.

## Big Picture

Player joins academy  
↓  
Supervisor registers player  
↓  
Class sheets generated (ATT_/ASS_)  
↓  
Coach records attendance / assessment  
↓  
Supervisor saves logs  
↓  
Monthly report & decisions

## Notes

- Registration creates the master player profile and links the player to a class.
- Attendance and assessment are recorded at each class session.
- Logs become the source of monthly reporting and coaching decisions.
